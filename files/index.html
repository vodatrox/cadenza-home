About Cadenza

Redefining HR as a Strategic Enabler of Business Excellence

Cadenza HR Consulting Ltd is a forward-thinking Human Resources and Business Consulting firm dedicated to empowering organizations with transformative workforce solutions.

Named after the musical term "Cadenza" — a powerful, masterful solo performance — our brand reflects the belief that every organization deserves to stand out by harnessing the strength of its people.

We combine data-driven insights, innovative HR solutions, and a deep understanding of workplace dynamics to help businesses create sustainable growth, enhance employee engagement, and build thriving workplace cultures.

What We Do

Comprehensive HR & Business Solutions

From talent acquisition to digital transformation, we provide end-to-end HR services tailored to your organization's unique needs.

Recruitment & Talent Acquisition

Executive search, headhunting, recruitment process outsourcing, and seamless onboarding and induction planning.

HR Analytics & Management

Workforce data analysis, HR technology integration, KPI optimization, and strategic HR metrics for informed decision-making.

Performance Management

Performance appraisal systems, goal setting, competency frameworks, and employee engagement and productivity enhancement.

Payroll & Compensation

Payroll processing, statutory compliance, tax management, salary benchmarking, benefits design, and retention strategies.

Learning & Development

Corporate training, leadership development, capacity-building programs, skill assessment, and career growth planning.

Compliance & Labor Law

HR policy alignment with labor laws, contract management, dispute resolution, workplace safety, and ethical practices.

HR Technology & Digital Transformation

HRIS and ERP implementation, AI-driven HR solutions, and end-to-end digital HR transformation consultancy.

HR Generalist & Consulting

Employee relations, workplace culture, organizational structure, change management, and business process optimization.

DEI & Career Transition

Inclusive workplace policies, unconscious bias training, career coaching, resume building, and outplacement services.
