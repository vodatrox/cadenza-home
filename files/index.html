Our Brief

HR That Works as Hard as You Do.

Cadenza Consulting is a Nigerian HR and business advisory firm dedicated to helping small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) unlock their full potential through strategic HR solutions.

We understand the realities of running a hands-on, blue-collar business — the late payrolls, staff turnover, compliance issues, and skill gaps.

That’s why we go beyond paperwork to deliver practical, human-centered solutions that boost performance, improve retention, and make your team proud to work with you.

Whether you run a fashion house, factory, logistics firm, or mechanic workshop — we’re here to manage your workforce while you grow your dream.

Got Questions about Cadenza? Look Here

Frequently asked questions about our services and how we can help grow your business

What HR services does Cadenza Consulting offer?

We provide end‑to‑end HR solutions, including:

  • Recruitment & Talent Acquisition.

  • Employee Management & Performance Appraisals.

  • Payroll Processing & Statutory Compliance.

  • HR Policy Development & Legal Advisory.

  • Learning, Development & Leadership Coaching.

  • HR Technology Implementation (HRIS, digital self‑service portals).

What types of businesses do you work with?

We specialize in small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), particularly blue‑collar and trade‑focused firms across Nigeria. Our solutions are tailored to industries such as manufacturing, logistics, construction, retail, and services.

How does your recruitment process work?

  • We conduct a needs analysis with you.

  • We source candidates via our network, job boards, and targeted outreach.

  • We screen, interview, and assess for skills and cultural fit.

  • We present you with a shortlist, coordinate interviews, and support onboarding.

How do I get started?

  • Contact us via WhatsApp or email to schedule a free discovery call.

  • We’ll conduct a brief needs analysis.

  • We’ll send a tailored proposal and service agreement.

  • Upon your approval, we begin transforming your HR functions.

Can you handle our payroll and statutory deductions?

Yes. We manage complete payroll processing—including PAYE, pension, NHF, and other statutory deductions—produce payslips, and ensure timely remittance to relevant authorities.

How much do your services cost?

Our core HR Outsourcing package starts at ₦150,000/month. Pricing may vary based on scope, additional site visits, or specialized projects. We provide a detailed proposal after an initial consultation.

How often will your team visit our site?

For our standard package, Cadenza staff visit your premises twice weekly. Additional visits can be arranged as needed.

What HR technology do you implement?

We deploy cloud‑based HRIS platforms that include:

  • Attendance & leave management

  • Employee self‑service portals

  • Performance tracking dashboards

  • Automated payroll and reporting modules

What makes Cadenza different from other HR firms?

  • Deep focus on blue‑collar SMEs in Nigeria

  • Hands‑on, humanized approach to employee management

  • Data‑driven HR analytics for actionable insights

  • Commitment to ethical practices and DEI
